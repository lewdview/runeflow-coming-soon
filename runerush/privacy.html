Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 29, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains the personal data we process, how we process it, and for what purposes.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Billing address (collected by our payment processor)
  • Purchase history and order details

Financial Information

  • Payment Processing: Credit card and payment information is processed securely by Stripe, our payment processor. We never store your complete credit card details on our servers.
  • Transaction Data: We store transaction IDs, purchase amounts, dates, and product types for order fulfillment and customer service.
  • Billing Information: Billing addresses are collected for tax calculation and compliance purposes.

Usage Data

  • Website analytics and user behavior
  • Download activity and access logs
  • Customer support interactions

How We Use Your Financial Information

We use your financial data for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Order Processing: To complete your purchases and deliver digital products
  • Customer Service: To handle refunds, disputes, and support requests
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with tax laws and financial regulations
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
  • Business Analytics: To analyze sales performance and improve our services (anonymized data only)
  • Communication: To send purchase confirmations, receipts, and important account updates

Financial Data Security

  • All payment processing is handled by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor
  • We use SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Financial data is stored with industry-standard security measures
  • Access to financial information is restricted to authorized personnel only

Third-Party Payment Processors

We use Stripe to process payments. When you make a purchase, your payment information is transmitted directly to Stripe and processed according to their privacy policy and security standards. We encourage you to review Stripe's privacy policy at https://stripe.com/privacy.

Data Retention

  • Transaction Records: Kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Customer Data: Retained as long as your account is active or as needed for business purposes
  • Marketing Data: Kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and financial data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your transaction history

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
